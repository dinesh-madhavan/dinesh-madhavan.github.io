Drop2Fill - Your Form-Filling Companion

Business Analysis UI/UX Browser Extension Productivity

Summary

Are you tired of repeatedly filling out the same information on job applications? Drop2Fill is a free browser extension for Safari and Chrome that saves your job history, educational background, skills, and personal details in a floating window. With intuitive drag-and-drop functionality, it lets you populate any form field in seconds, streamlining the application process and saving you time and energy.

Visit drop2fill.com to download the extension.

Ideation

The idea for Drop2Fill emerged from personal frustration with repeatedly entering identical details across countless job portals. Existing autofill tools either lacked Safari support, charged subscription fees, or harvested personal data. I envisioned a lightweight, secure, and completely free solution that prioritizes user privacy and simplicity.

How It Works

  • Floating Window: Store your details—job history, education, skills, and more—in a convenient sidebar.
  • Drag-and-Drop: Simply drag any saved entry into the corresponding form field on any application page.
  • Seamless Integration: Automatically detects and adapts to a wide range of form fields across different websites.
  • Local Secure Storage: All data is stored locally on your device, ensuring your personal information never leaves your control.
  • Completely Free: No hidden fees or subscriptions—Drop2Fill is always free to use.
